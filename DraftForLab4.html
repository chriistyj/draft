Discord easter egg

Discord has always been hiding secret in the app without being noticed for so long.

Empathy banana

When your search leads to no results, Discord will sometimes display an "empathy banana" instead of the usual no results dialogue.

Broken magnifying glass

When you have no results in a search (Similar to the empathy banana), there will be a broken magnifying glass on rare occasions (or if you search in a channel without Message History permissions)

Computer Man

Visiting printer.discord.com will redirect you to a video clip from an old Canadian show called Vid Kids. Discord developer b1nzy implemented this Easter egg.
